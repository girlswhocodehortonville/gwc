Depression is a mental disorder that can develope from certain stimuli, traumatic events being the most popular of these stimuli. Depression results in unnessesary sadness, extreme mood swings, and many physical effects, like fatigue, loss of sleep, headaches, backpain, and loss of appetite. Depression can occur at any age, and over 30,000 cases of suicide result from this incurable metal issue every year.
