Happy Beats

Durham University study proved that sad music brought most listeners comfort. People tend to turn to sad music in low points of their lives, like a loss of a loved one or an ending relationship

  • A 2013 study found that those who listened to upbeat music had improved moods and happiness in 14 days
  • Soothing, upbeat music paired with meditation can ease anxiety and depression moods (calming music along with cognitive therapy is more  effective in lowering anxiety than conventional therapy alone)
  • In a study of 144 students, college students said pop, rock, oldies, and classical music helped them feel "happier, and more optimistic, friendly, relaxed and calm."

  • Calming music can promote good social behavior and decreases impulsive behaviors
  • Music is so effective in reducing anxiety that its used frequently in dental or other therapy setting to help patients cope with worries of procedures
  • Music soothes patients with critical injuries or sicknesses, such as cancer, burns, etc
  • Music is also used to calm anxious feeling in elderly people, new mothers, and children
